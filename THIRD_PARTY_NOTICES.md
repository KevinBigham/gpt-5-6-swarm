- routing among GPT-5.6 Sol, Terra, and Luna with explicit effort levels;
- self-contained worker briefs and checkable artifact handoffs;

- a single writer per checkout;

Material additions in Swarm include:

- coordinator-owned dependency graphs and bounded fan-out/fan-in scheduling;
- worker and peak-concurrency budgets;
- action classes, task fingerprints, launch nonces, and a complete execution-state model;
- disjoint worktree/resource ownership and a single integration lease;
- duplicate-launch recovery, cancellation, drift detection, and unknown-writer handling;
- two-stage arming for one-shot actions;
- task-specific route templates and structured progress/final receipts.
